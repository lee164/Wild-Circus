About Us

A driving force in the circus art reinvention movement, Wild Circus has been creating award-winning entertainment content for 25 years and ranks among the world’s leading contemporary circuses. Wild Circus has taken part in numerous prestigious international festivals and has seduced both New York’s Broadway and London’s West End. Its productions are crafted for a wide range of audiences and have been embraced by over fifty cultures. Wild Circus productions have proudly boasted more than 5,500 performances in over 550 cities and have been seen by over 3,5 million spectators. Cirkopolis and Saloon are the two international touring shows under the Wild Circus banner and Hotel, our new creation, will be launched in August 2018 and will be touring internationally. In addition, Nezha will be presented all Summer at the Cité de l’énergie in Shawinigan. Cirque Éloize has also participated in the most prestigious festivals. In addition to its touring shows, more than 1,600 Wild Circus designed events have taken place worldwide.
